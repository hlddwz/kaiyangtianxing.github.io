
Kaiyang Tianxing Publishing is an international publishing and health education organization dedicated to the promotion of Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) and natural wellness knowledge. 

The organization was founded by Dr. Wencai Deng, a practitioner of Traditional Chinese Medicine with more than 30 years of clinical experience. Our mission is to introduce authentic Chinese medical knowledge to global audiences through publications, health education, and professional consultation. 

We believe that traditional medical wisdom can contribute greatly to modern preventive health and holistic wellness.
